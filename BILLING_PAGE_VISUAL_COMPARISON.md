## Implementation Strategy

### Why Option A (Display Both) vs Other Options?

**Option A: Display both charges and payments** ‚úÖ CHOSEN
- ‚úÖ Non-invasive (doesn't modify data)
- ‚úÖ Transparent (shows exactly what's in database)
- ‚úÖ Safe (no risk of data corruption)
- ‚úÖ Immediate (no migration needed)

**Option B: Data migration to fix order_lines**
- ‚ùå Risky (could corrupt data)
- ‚ùå Complex (need to recalculate prices)
- ‚ùå Time-consuming (need testing)
- ‚ùå Irreversible (hard to undo)

**Option C: Recalculate on display**
- ‚ùå Complex logic
- ‚ùå Performance impact
- ‚ùå Might not match payment records
- ‚ùå Doesn't explain discrepancies
